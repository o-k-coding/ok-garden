Kesh is a black furred Tabaxi male that resembles a panther. He is taller than the average human with a sleek and agile build. His golden eyes that seem to hold secrets. 

Kesh is enigmatic and charming and runs a spice stand at the Kalenel markets. For some though, he will also deal in information.

Remember, spice and secrets go hand in hand! 🌶️🗝️
